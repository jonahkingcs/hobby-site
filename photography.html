Photography

Wherever I go my camera comes with me. I love to take photos out in nature or on trips to exciting cities. My favourites are photographs of friends and family associated with good memories. Most of my photos are taken using my phone for convenience but I found a bargain on eBay for a Canon EOS 4000D for more professional photography. I also nicked my parents old Panasonic DMC-TZ10 digital camera from 2010 since it was only sat in a box collecting dust. It gives any image taken with flash a great vintage look, reminding me of old family photo albums.

Cascade Mountain, Banff - July 2024

All of Banff's streets pointing North have the most breathtaking view of Cascade Mountain. I really loved the view from this back alley in particular. It adds a nice contrast seeing nature towering over the built-up area, completly overshadowing manmade construction.

Shot on: iPhone 15 Pro

Cadbury World, Bournville, Birmingham - June 2025

My friend Will spotting the big Cadbury sign across the canal in Bournville. This was taken on the first evening after final exams and we were finished for the summer. We spent the whole evening exploring all of the parks we hadn't ever been to around our area back when it was still light until 9pm.
