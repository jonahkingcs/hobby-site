Photography

Wherever I go my camera comes with me. I love to take photos out in nature or on trips to exciting cities. My favourites are photographs of friends and family associated with good memories. Most of my photos are taken using my phone for convenience but I found a bargain on eBay for a Canon EOS 4000D for more professional photography.
